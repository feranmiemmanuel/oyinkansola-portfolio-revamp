Who I Am

I'm a passionate UI/UX designer with over 3 years of experience creating meaningful digital experiences. My journey started with a curiosity about how people create and design websites and games, and evolved into a mission to make digital products more human-centered.

I believe great design happens when empathy meets functionality. Every project begins with understanding user needs and business goals, then crafting solutions that are both beautiful and purposeful.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me reading a book or watching movies. I also enjoy watching videos on TikTok and catching up with the latest design trends on X.

My Approach

01

Discover & Understand

Every project begins with clarity. I define the problem, align with business goals, and gather user insights through research, stakeholder conversations, and competitive analysis.

02

Plan & Ideate

With a clear understanding of the challenge, I map user journeys, create user flows, and explore design directions. I sketch, wireframe, and build structure, focusing on usability and accessibility.

03

Design & Prototype

I bring ideas to life through high-fidelity designs that reflect brand personality and visual clarity. Interactive prototypes help test functionality and user experience before development.

04

Refine & Deliver

I test, gather feedback, and iterate on designs. Once finalized, I ensure smooth handoff to developers with detailed specs and documentation.
